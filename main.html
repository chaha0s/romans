OUR STORY

It’s 2020, and Rohan and Mansi are two MBA students navigating Con Week on Zoom. Mansi claims it was ‘crush at first sight,’. Rohan? He’s not buying it for a second.

Their banter soon turned into study sessions: he’d decode Decision Sciences, and she’d pretend to teach him Accounts. Rohan nailed both and Mansi happily took the credit as his “brilliant” teacher.

By March 2021, Mansi (aka the braver one) asked him out. Rohan has a totally fictional memory of it being the other way around, but we all know who the real boss is.

Fast forward to now: their story has grown from a chance Zoom meeting to a lifelong partnership, and they can’t wait to start this next chapter together!
